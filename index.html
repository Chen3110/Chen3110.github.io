About Me

My name is Anjun Chen. I am currently pursuing my Doctor’s degree in control engineering with Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the supervision of Qi Ye. I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in communication engineering from Jilin University in 2019.

Research Interest

My research interests lie in 3D human body reconstruction. I’ve been learning how to reconstruct 3D human body mesh from mmWave radar signals.
