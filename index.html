Anjun Chen

My name is Anjun Chen. I am a last-year Ph.D. candidate in the College of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the supervision of Prof. Qi Ye and Prof. Jiming Chen at PerPLe and NeSC Group. I am currently a visiting student in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania working with Prof. Lingjie Liu at Graphics Lab and GRASP Lab. I am expected to obtain my Ph.D. degeree in July, 2025.

My research interests lie in Computer Vision and my research works focus on multi-modal multi-view data fusion for 3D human reconstruction.
