My name is Anjun Chen. I am currently pursuing my Doctor's degree with electronic information in Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the supervision of Prof. Qi Ye and Prof. Jiming Chen. I obtained my Bachelor's degree with communication engineering from Jilin University in 2019.

Research Interest

My research interests lie in Computer Vision. My previous work focused on adaptive multi-modal multi-view data fusion for 3D human body reconstruction. My current research plan is to achieve feature alignment among multi-modal data.
