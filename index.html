Anjun Chen 陈安军

About Me

My name is Anjun Chen. I am currently pursuing my Doctor's degree with control engineering in Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the supervision of Prof. Qi Ye and Prof. Jiming Chen. I obtained my Bachelor's degree with communication engineering from Jilin University in 2019.

Research Interest

My research interests lie in 3D human body reconstruction and multi-sensor fusion. My current research focuses on adaptive multi-modal multi-view fusion for 3D human body reconstruction.
