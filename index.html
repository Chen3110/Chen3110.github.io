I am currently an Assistant Professor in the School of Informatics at Xiamen University at Human+ Lab. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in the College of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the supervision of Prof. Qi Ye and Prof. Jiming Chen at PerPLe and NeSC Group. I was a visiting student in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania working with Prof. Lingjie Liu at Graphics Lab and GRASP Lab. My research interests lie in Robotics and Human Computer Interaction, especially focusing on Enbodied AI, Humanoid Robots, and Human Motion Capture.
