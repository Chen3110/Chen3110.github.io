About Me

My name is Anjun Chen. I am currently pursuing my Master’s degree with Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, under the supervision of Qi Ye. I obtained my Bachelor’s degree from Jilin University in 2019.

Research Interest

My research interests lie in machine learning on millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar sensor and 3D vision. I’ve researched how to recognize hand gesture from mmWave radar signals. I’ve been learning how to apply radar signal processing to 3D vision problems.
